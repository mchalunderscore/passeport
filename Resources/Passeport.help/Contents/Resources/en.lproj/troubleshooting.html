Troubleshooting

An integration needs repair

Unlock Passeport and choose Repair. Repair recreates Passeport-managed files while preserving unrelated commands and configuration.

A test fails

Confirm the app is running and unlocked, approve the requested operation, and retry. If the identity changed, repair the integration first.

Collect diagnostics

In Settings, choose Copy Diagnostics. Diagnostics contain app and integration state but exclude keys, recovery words, passwords, and operation contents.

Command not found

Ensure ~/.local/bin is on the shell’s PATH, or invoke the installed passeport-* command by its full path.
