Security and locking

The seed is stored in a private on-disk vault that can optionally be encrypted with a password. Lock clears the seed from memory. A protected vault requires its password when unlocking; an unprotected vault unlocks directly.

Approval controls

Confirm each signature or decryption shows the requesting client and operation before private-key use.

Auto-lock

Passeport can lock when the screen locks or sleeps and after a configurable period of inactivity.

The optional vault password is chosen during identity creation or restoration and is not part of key derivation.
