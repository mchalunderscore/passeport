Getting started

Create or restore an identity

Choose Create New Identity to generate a random identity, or Import Recovery Phrase to restore the same identity from its 24 words. You can optionally encrypt the on-disk identity vault with a password.

Verify the backup

Open Backup & Recovery and choose Verify Recovery Phrase. Passeport asks for four randomly selected words without copying or displaying the full phrase.

Configure a tool

Unlock Passeport, open Integrations, select a backend where applicable, and choose Configure. Use Test afterward to perform a real cryptographic round trip.
