Backup and recovery

The 24-word recovery phrase recreates the identity and all of its keys.

  • Write the words down in order and store them offline.
  • Do not keep screenshots or clipboard copies.
  • Verify the phrase after creating it and periodically afterward.
  • Keep the OpenPGP revocation certificate with your backup.

Restore

Choose Restore, enter all 24 words, optionally choose a local vault password, review the replacement warning, and confirm. Repair installed integrations afterward so they publish the restored identity’s current public material.
